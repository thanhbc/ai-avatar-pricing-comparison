Interpretation

At high utilization (near 24/7 speaking), Tencent’s flat monthly fee scales linearly with concurrency and is dramatically cheaper than per-minute billing. As talk-time ratio drops, Heygen costs fall proportionally and can undercut Tencent for low-usage fleets.

Reference pricing

  • Heygen API: credits ⇒ speaking minutes (e.g., 1 credit = 5 minutes). Pro ≈ $0.198/min, Scale ≈ $0.10/min. Official page.
  • Tencent IVH Interactive: monthly per concurrency path (2D: $500, 3D: $800). See Tencent Cloud “AI Digital Human” pricing/guide.

Tip: For your Jewel kiosk POC, start with 2D Tencent for fixed-cost lanes, and use Heygen for pre-rendered explainers (cheaper per rendered minute). Tune Talk time & Concurrency to match footfall.
